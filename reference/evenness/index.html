(un)Evenness

dissimilarity (distribution, classes=None)

$$D_{\alpha \beta} = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{t=1}^T \left| \frac{n_\alpha(t)}{N_\alpha} - \frac{n_\beta(t)}{N_\beta} \right|$$
